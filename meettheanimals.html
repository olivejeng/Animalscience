Meet our Animals!

These animals live in our lab, and are well taken care by the students everyday!

Mammals

Jazz

Jazz is one of our cats! She is a queen and loves to be carried around the lab

Amber

Amber is our second cat! She is an independent gal and spends her day sleeping next to the heat lamps around the lab!

Oreo

Oreo is our guinea pig! She loves to squeak and munch on carrots!

Tink

Tink is our only ferret in our lab. She is a limp noodle and a very silly goose (not to be confused with a limp silly goose)

Rats

We have many different rat cages. They are all tame, and some are even trained to do fun little tricks! This photo is of Rosemary from Cage C. She is trained to go through obstacle courses!

Tsaro

Tsaro is a sugar glider! He is an elderly man and is almost 17! He loves to spend his day sleeping in his pouch.

Mice

We have about 19 cages of mice in our lab. They are mainly used as food for other animals, but we love them anyways!

Spiny Mice

Spiny mice are desert animals and need heat to survive. They love to do backflips in thier holding cages!

Toffee

Toffee is a wiggly baby who enjoys a good head scratch.

Pepper

Pepper is one of our calmest rabbits. He loves being held and will sit with you all day!

Mulberry

Mulberry is a sweet baby angel and will come up to you for nose rubs! He is being traned to stand on his hind legs!

Cotton

Cotton is our only Netherland Dwarf rabbit. He is very fluffy and LOVES going on walks outside!
