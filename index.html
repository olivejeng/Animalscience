About Us

From the playful antics of our resident cockatoos to the gentle nuzzling of affectionate cats, each day offers new opportunities for students to engage with a diverse range of species. They delve into the intracacies of animal behavior, training, welfare and anatomy as well as first aid and grooming. These subjects are covered in both the Veterinary and Small Animal Care classes.
